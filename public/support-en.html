Splicon Support

Split View Icon Maker for iPad

About the App

Splicon lets you create custom launcher icons for iPad Split View. Combine the icons of two apps to generate a shortcut icon for your Home Screen that opens both apps side by side with one tap.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How many times can I use it for free?

You can save up to 3 icons for free. Additional generations require upgrading to Splicon Pro.

Q. How do I restore my purchase?

Tap "Restore Purchase" on the Pro upgrade screen. As long as you use the same Apple ID, Pro features will be restored on any device.

Q. Where are generated icons saved?

Icons are saved to your device's photo library. You can find them in the Photos app.

Q. I'm not sure how to set up the Shortcut.

After saving your icon, follow the in-app Setup Guide. You can also copy the step-by-step instructions to your clipboard for easy reference in the Shortcuts app.

Q. Which devices are supported?

Splicon supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 17.0 or later.
