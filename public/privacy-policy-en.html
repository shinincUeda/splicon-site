Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2025

Splicon (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information.

  • No account registration is required.
  • App search uses Apple's iTunes Search API. Search queries are sent directly to Apple's servers and are never stored or accessed by the developer.
  • All icon generation is performed locally on your device. No image data is transmitted to external servers.

2. Photo Library Access

The App requests write access to your photo library solely to save generated icon images. This access is used only for saving icons — the App does not read or access any existing photos.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers an in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. All transactions are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. The developer never receives or stores any payment information.

4. Data Storage

  • App settings and generation history are stored only in local storage on your device.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not share, sell, or otherwise disclose any user data to third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time without prior notice. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out.
